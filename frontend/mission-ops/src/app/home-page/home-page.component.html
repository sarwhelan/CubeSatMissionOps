What is the CubeSat?

CPSX received a grant from the Canadian Space Agency (CSA) to fund the development of a CubeSat device. The CubeSat is a miniature satellite set to be launched in 2021. The device will be designed and built by various teams composed of Western University students and faculty members. Once the device has been launched, mission control will be able to operate the device from a mission control application detailed in this document. This section of the document contains introductory information about what the purpose of the application will be, as well as definitions, acronyms, abbreviations, and document conventions.
